Personal Bio:

My name is Aurelien Delaguillaumie. I am 30 years old and I live in Dublin since 8 years. Before coming to Ireland I lived in France, near Grenoble and in New Caledonia, a small pacific island located on the east coast of Australia. I have been working in IT for the past 7 years, across several technical support roles. This has motivated me to develop programming skills and pushed me to attend a part-time Bachelor in computer science at Griffith College.
